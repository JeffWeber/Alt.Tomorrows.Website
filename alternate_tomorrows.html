About Alternate Tomorrows

Alternate Tomorrows is a collection of short fiction set in futures that science hasn't ruled out.

These aren't stories about faster-than-light travel or alien civilizations. They're about the quieter strangeness closer at hand — the researcher who finds nine missing seconds in a signal from Titan, the cartographer racing to redraw coastlines before they go to print, the field scientist watching a flower bloom in January.

Each story begins with something plausible. What happens next is fiction. The line between the two is the point.

New stories are added when they're ready. Each one gets a number. That's all.
