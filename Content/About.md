**Alternate Tomorrows**

These are first-person accounts from people living inside futures we're building right now.

Not space operas. Not thought experiments from a distance. Each story drops you into a single life — a narrator's body, their habits, their specific corner of a world that doesn't quite match the one we know yet. You feel the weight of the air they breathe. You understand what they've lost before you understand why.

The futures here aren't invented. They're extrapolated — from the science on the table, the systems already in motion, the trajectories that serious people are quietly tracking. The fiction is what happens to the person inside them.

Read them here. Listen on the podcast. Get them delivered by newsletter. New ones are added when they're ready. Each gets a number.

These stories and their worlds are brought to life with a custom story engine. AI generates the drafts. I edit the outputs if needed, and decide what gets released.
