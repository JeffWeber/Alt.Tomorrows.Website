**Alternate Tomorrows**

These are first-person accounts from people living inside futures we're building right now.

Not space operas. Not abstract hypotheticals. Each story drops you into a single life: a narrator's body, their habits, their specific corner of a world that doesn't quite match the one we know. You feel the weight of the air they breathe. You understand what they've lost before you understand why.

The futures here aren't invented. They're extrapolated from the science on the table, the systems already in motion, and the trajectories that serious people are quietly tracking. I wanted to know what those futures felt like from the inside.

To find out, I built a custom story engine. The engine generates an extrapolated world, then finds someone already living inside it and lets them talk. I listen. I edit the outputs when needed, and I decide what gets released.

Read them here. Listen on the podcast. Get them delivered by newsletter. New stories are added when they’re ready.
