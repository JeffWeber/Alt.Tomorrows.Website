The Archival Premise

History is merely the timeline that survived. For every decision made, every technological breakthrough achieved, and every crisis narrowly averted, infinite other realities branched off into the dark.

Alternate Tomorrows is an archival project dedicated to exploring those divergent paths. Grounded in real scientific research and speculative analysis, we document what could be—or what is, somewhere else.

The Methodology

Each case file in the archive represents a rigorously constructed projection of a specific divergence point in human history or future development. These are not mere fantasies; they are logical extrapolations meticulously compiled into structured timelines, sensory data profiles, and key historical events.

We invite you to spelunk these timelines. To examine the metadata, read the field notes, and immerse yourself in the environments of worlds that parallel our own.
