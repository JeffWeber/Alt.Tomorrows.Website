About Alternate Tomorrows

Scientific discoveries, technological innovation, and societal change are sprinting forward at an exponential rate, driven largely by advances in AI.

I've been obsessed with exploring the effects of this accelerated movement on humanity at both the individual and societal scale.

As these breakthroughs arrive, how will they actually play out? What will our world look like 10, 20, 50, or 100 years from now? How will daily life actually feel?

The best way I've found to explore these "tomorrows" is through raw, first-person accounts of people living in them. These stories and vignettes act as close-up views into highly plausible futures.

These personal accounts show the good, the bad, and the ugly of what we likely have to look forward to.

If you want to experience what tomorrow holds for us, you've come to the right place.
