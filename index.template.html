Divergent Timelines

What is a Divergent Timeline?

A single point of failure. A pathogen mutates. A current shifts. A crop collapses. Then the second-order consequences begin — the ones nobody modeled — and the world restructures itself around what's left.

Each timeline traces one scientifically plausible divergence through decades of cascading consequence, arriving at a future that is not dystopia and not utopia — just a different normal, built on the rubble of a specific loss.
